Warming & Cooling Centers

The web application was built by Tom Kompare as a public service. Even though this web application uses the latest information published by the City of Chicago, please phone ahead to the centers (or 311) before travelling. Hours of operation may change or may be extended during extreme weather events. Some centers may be closed on holidays.
